## 1. Nature of the prior item

The only prior public version of this work is a **preprint posted to arXiv** (arXiv:2606.14589),
an open preprint server. It has **not** undergone peer review and has **not** been published in
any peer-reviewed conference or journal. Under IEEE's policy on preprints, posting to arXiv is
permitted and does not constitute prior publication. It is disclosed here in the interest of full
transparency.

## 2. Relationship between the two versions

Both versions derive from the **same underlying study**: an eight-week longitudinal analysis of
22 silent-failure incident postmortems in one continuously operating production LLM agent runtime.
They share the study's data, the central concept *fail-plausible*, and the core empirical findings.

## 3. How this IEEE Software submission differs from the arXiv preprint

- **Length and format.** The arXiv preprint is a full-length (~8,000-word) academic paper with
  nine sections. This IEEE Software submission is a **substantially condensed (~3,400-word)
  feature-length experience report** conforming to IEEE Software's format (≤4,200 words, ≤15
  references).
- **Framing and audience.** The preprint is framed as an academic taxonomy for the research
  community, with an extended related-work survey, a formal case-study methodology, and a full
  threats-to-validity treatment. This version is **reframed as a practitioner experience report
  ("lessons learned")** for software-engineering practitioners, foregrounding actionable guidance.
- **New material added for IEEE Software.** Three explicit, bullet-listed **practitioner
  takeaways** (as required by IEEE Software); a tightened narrative centered on the most
  transferable practices; and practitioner-oriented framing throughout.
- **Material condensed or omitted.** Relative to the preprint, the extended related-work survey,
  the full formal methodology and inter-annotator discussion, the complete per-incident catalog,
  and the full threats-to-validity section are condensed or omitted to fit the magazine format.

## 4. Citation

The arXiv preprint is **referenced in the IEEE Software manuscript** as the extended version of
the work, so readers can locate the full-length treatment and the complete incident corpus.
